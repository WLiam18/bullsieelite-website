About BullsieElite

Hitting the mark, every time

Founded in 2023 in Chennai, BullsieElite emerged from a simple belief: Indian startups and SMEs deserve world-class technology solutions without the enterprise price tag.

Our name reflects our mission to be like a bullseye on a target, we focus on precision, accuracy, and hitting your business goals dead center. We've helped over 50+ companies transform their digital presence, from early-stage startups to established businesses scaling their operations.

What sets us apart is our deep understanding of the Indian market. We build solutions that work on 3G connections, integrate seamlessly with local payment gateways like Razorpay and Paytm, and are designed for Indian user behavior and preferences.
